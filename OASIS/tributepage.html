SACHIN TENDULKAR

GOD OF THE CRICKET

Biography

Sachin Tendulkar is one of India's most popular and prized cricketers and is famous worldwide. He has a wide fan following which is not restricted only to India. He is a skilled batsman who is also called Master blaster or even God of cricket by his fellow cricketers and fans globally. His full name is Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar, and he was born on 24th April 1973 in Mumbai. He has been interested in cricket since he was in school and made his international debut in 1989. His story of progress and training is very interesting and inspiring to many.
